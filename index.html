About

BarFly is a company catered to people having a great time with their favorite drinks. Founded in 2020 by Ivan Mallory and Ismet Uner, BarFly is a mixology service that can be booked at the touch of your fingers. When you submit a request for your special occasion, BarFly will send a certified mixologist to create your favorite drinks. Our mixologists will walk you through every step of making your favorite drink so you can ultimately become the mixologist!
